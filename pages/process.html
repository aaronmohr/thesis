Jan 2018 — Project Manifesto

Early on in the process, I started to develop a sort of project manifesto to abide by throughout the creative process. These ideas are an abstract overview of how I want to define and approach this project. Albeit poetic in nature, I want to use this manifesto as a reference to keep myself on track creatively.

"Rosy Glow is a multimedia music project by Aaron Mohr. The concept of the project focuses around capturing the essence of the in-between state. A Sunrise and a sunset, the soft glow of light, the digital meets the analog, so on and so forth. All of these ideas fall under the Rosy Glow aesthetic and are meant to work cohesively to produce a fully resolved collection of art and design.

Everything must be cohesive and abide by the RG brand guidelines. A visual system will be developed in order to ensure that the project feels complete. While the focus of the project lies primarily on the musical output, Rosy Glow is meant to be an overarching concept in which various creative outputs can live together under one roof.

Rosy — Promising of suggesting good fortune or happiness; hopeful.”
